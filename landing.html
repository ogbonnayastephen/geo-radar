The process

From invisible to cited in four steps

GEO Radar runs the complete AEO audit pipeline — discovery, crawl, citation check, and fix — in a single session.

1

Discover

Scrape real queries from Google autocomplete and Reddit, clustered by Claude into buyer, partner, and media intent categories

2

Match

Breadth-first site crawl maps every page. Claude matches each query to the most semantically relevant page on your site

3

Check

Live API calls to Perplexity Sonar, OpenAI gpt-4o-search-preview, and Gemini Search Grounding. Real citations, real results

4

Fix

Page-level content rewrite, question-phrased headings, and validated FAQPage schema — ready to implement, not just diagnose
